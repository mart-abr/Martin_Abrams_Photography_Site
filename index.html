About

My name is Martin. I am an Australian photographer living in Japan. Photography has been both my passion and creative outlet, allowing me to capture moments that reflect the beauty of both cultures I call home. Living in Japan has given me the opportunity to explore a unique blend of traditional and modern landscapes, from serene temples and mountains to the vibrant energy of city life. My Australian roots, however, continue to inspire me with a love for wide-open spaces, natural light, and storytelling through images. Through my work, I aim to share a perspective shaped by two worlds - the quiet elegance of Japan and the raw beauty of Australia.
